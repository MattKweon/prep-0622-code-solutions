Indiana Jones

Raiders of the Lost Arc

Lucas concieved Raiders of the Lost Ark in the early 1920s. Seeking to modernize the serial films of the early 20th-century, he developed the idea further with kaufman, who suggested the Ark as the films's goal. Lucas eventually focused on developing his 1977 space opera Star Wars.

Temple of Doom

The film was released to financial success but initial reviews were mixed, criticizing it's darker elements and violence. However critical opinion has improved since 1984, citing the film's intensity and imagination.

The Last Crusade

After the moxed reaction to Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom, Speilberg chose to tone down the dark tone and graphic violence in the next installment.
